The Second Pillar of [[Historian Syncretate]], Child of [[Amaunator]], the religious focal point for the majority of Historia. Presides in the [[Cathedral of the Dawn]], and is considered the political superior to the [[Vox Platini Dei]]. Mandated that a routine tithe be given to the [[Church of the Golden Sun]] by the [[Church of the Platinum Lord]] in an attempt to keep the Vox Platini in a submissive role. They preach that by the grace of Amaunator the peoples of Historia will grow and come to rule [[Akran]], "for everything His Golden Light touches belongs to His children".
