## Tenets

Justice: Followers of the Church of the Platinum Lord believe in upholding the principles of justice with unwavering dedication. They are committed to ensuring fairness, righteousness, and the rule of law in all aspects of life. It is their sacred duty to be champions of justice and to stand against injustice wherever it may manifest.

Unity: The Church teaches that unity and harmony are essential virtues. Followers are called to foster cooperation and understanding among all beings, transcending differences and divisions. They believe that through unity, society can flourish, and individuals can find strength in togetherness.

Courage: The Church of the Platinum Lord emphasizes the importance of confronting darkness and malevolence with unwavering courage and zeal. Followers are encouraged to be beacons of light in times of adversity, to stand resolute against evil, and to work tirelessly to dispel shadows wherever they may lurk.
