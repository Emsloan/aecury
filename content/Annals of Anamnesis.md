Archives hidden deep below the main compound.  As you spiral down the stair, shelf after shelf of precious stone. Thin veils of water that come from the skylight, fall delicately as if a mist blown, down the shelves. Every so often, as it random, there are small recess that carve into the walls where more gems reside. There are gems of amethyst, jade, amber, emerald, and ruby. As you reach the base the material primarily turns to well hewn diamond and shards of Topaz. The halls spiral deeper and deeper to a depth of 300 feet where then you find a surface of smooth alabaster. there in all directions you see hall that delve farther than you can see into the pitch black. There seems to be hundreds of feet of stacked gems each glowing with the faintest soft light from within.
Living here is an ancient topaz dragon who sacrificed size and strength for fractionable memory and histories of the world. hopefully a cool npc
