A small dome shaped mushroom that has a white exterior that is spotted with faint blue circles. This mushroom is harvested and consumed by the people of [[Abydos]]  during the [[Gaze of Osiris]] . These mushrooms have a psychedelic affect that wears off after roughly five hours.
