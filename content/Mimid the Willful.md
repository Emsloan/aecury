Mimid the Willful is the youngest of the Intrinsics and perhaps the most powerful in terms of direct impact on existence. Mimid embodies willpower, choice, and personal agency. Most crucially, Mimid sacrificed themselves to grant all creatures the gift of free will—opening their own wrists to shed their divine essence so that mortal and immortal beings alike could determine their own destinies.

## Nature

Willfulness in Mimid's case means the capacity to choose, to desire, and to act according to one's own nature. This Intrinsic represents agency and the power of personal determination.

## The Greatest Sacrifice

When Inferniax created his clay vessels but found them lacking will and agency, he entreated Mimid to infuse his creations with the power to choose. Mimid, in an act that may have been rebellion against their ordained purpose or born of empathy, did far more than was asked.

Mimid opened their wrists and let their hot blood flow freely onto the clay creations. The nascent beings gorged themselves on Mimid's essence—an amalgam of pure desire and will. As Mimid's blood suffused their bodies, it became their own essence, their own will. In this way, Mimid's sacrifice granted free will to all creatures.

## Pact

Before fading, Mimid made a pact with the nascent elves: Regardless of time or circumstance, Mimid's domain would remain their own. The gift of will cannot be truly taken away.

## Legacy

Mimid's sacrifice is fundamental to all existence on Aecury. Every creature with agency, every being capable of choice and desire, carries within them a fragment of Mimid's essence.
