The capital city of the [[Sandswimmers]]. The site of where the Sandswimmers first came after their diaspora from their ancestral homelands. Mawa has grown large thanks to the protection of the [[Etched]] and now struggles to feed its populace. Some families have gone back to the tunnels and the greater underdark.
