Mystra is a goddess of creation, magic, and matter, dwelling within [[The Spire]] as one of [[Ao]]'s chosen and most powerful servants. She represents the divine aspects of creative power, arcane knowledge, and the fundamental forces that constitute existence.

## Divine Domains

- Creation
- Magic
- Matter
- The Weave

## Role

As one of Ao's chosen deities, Mystra works to maintain the balance of creation and magic across Aecury. She is the patroness of all mages, wizards, and those who seek to understand the fundamental forces of reality.

## Dwelling

Mystra resides in The Spire, the intermediate divine realm that serves as home to Ao's most powerful and trusted servants.
