An exceedingly rare material with numerous magical properties. It is a silvery metal with that is very reflective with slowly undulating multicolored patterns constantly in flux on it's surface. It is surprisingly low density.

It is has been used as a vital component in the creation of numerous magical tools and artifacts. Artificer guilds closely guard techniques used to draw out it's magical properties. It can be used to detect, dispel, store, and focus arcane energies.
