When his grandfather passed he left cayden with the advice " dont get stuck here, take your skills that I have given you and make a name for yourself." Taking this advice to heart, Cayden began to explore the lands of [[EverRealm]] . Traveling from city to city he quickly became dissatisfied by the lack of variety he found on the hunt. On the seventh of April, Cayden Sinclair boarded the Grey Winds bound for the New Land leaving behind his family in search for adventure, and fortunes. 
   After a two month voyage he arrived to find the island was more primitive and dangerous than he could have imagined, he settled down in the city of Iron Bay and used his skills as a hunter to track down new prey and sell skins and resources back to the home lands.  Work was slow at first, as he stayed away from the overly dangerous hunts while getting accustomed to the region. He made enough not to starve but was affording little of the comfort he was used to growing up.  

   After only a week and a half he decided he was going to try his hand at bigger game, and began a hunt for Saber tooth Tiger. He was hunting for two days before coming across the beast in a gully, clearly injured, and roaring at something Cayden couldn't see.   Using the natural cover to sneak around to the side of the tiger he notched an arrow took aim and waited. Suddenly it leapt, striking a man in the nearby brush. A stranger fell to the ground sporting a large gash in his abdomen and Cayden let his arrow fly. It caught the beast through it's neck and it flailed around for a short minute before falling dead. 

    Left with the choice of collecting a substantial bounty and a chance to clean up and rest easy, or the option to use what little supplies he had to save this man, he opted to take the man back to civilization.He later learned the man was Rylo King, a man 5 years his senior, who had been hunting the island for months. Rylo swore a life debt to Cayden;  as payment Cayden's request was that when Rylo is well, the two hunt together and Rylo teaches him everything he's learned. 

     Cayden had a new found respect of this island scared into him after the Saber tooth tiger hunt and decided to stick to the smaller hunts while Rylo was on the mend.  After a month of scraping by he and Rylo went on their first joint hunt, an Aurochs and together they managed to put it down without sustaining any injuries. It was finally enough gold to get a good hot meal, a bath, and some new leather. They hunted together for several months, both paying and earning life debts more times than they could count. At last count Cayden claimed to be up one, Rylo claimed he was up two.  

    Together they had been making enough money from their trade that Cayden was able to open up a lodge. He made contracts with the sailors and traders and would post the going rate on hides bones and even certain native flora. He would take 10 percent of the gold for use of his contracts and information, the lodge took 10 percent,  and Rylo took 5 more.  It took a while for the system to catch but by the middle of March they had 5 members. Their newest 2 Karn, and Ike were a couple of Dwarf brothers. They managed to find clever ways to trap certain creatures and the two of them single handedly developed the live trade. Business was thriving, and after a rough start Cayden found able to afford to build an lavish cabin. It was more of an improved hunting lodge with a bedroom on the top floor than it was a cabin, but it was Cayden's an he was proud of the life he was making. 

    With business growing, Cayden was starting to feel like he had made it big, and the excitement was getting to his head. On a late November night, feeling invincible, he invited Rylo on a larger than usual hunt. Rylo was hesitant at first but eventually caved to Cayden's charisma. They tracked the fabled Were-Tiger for a week through the foothills of the The Death Spines trying to plan the best method of attack. On one night during their stake out Cayden fell asleep during his watch and allowed the Were-Tiger to get the jump on them. He woke briefly to a kick smashing into his side, throwing him into a tree. The commotion was enough to wake Rylo who had so far remained unnoticed. Seeing the Were-Tiger charging toward Cayden he grabbed his bow as quickly as he could and shouted toward the Were-Tiger. He notched two arrows, and as the creature turned it's head to look, he let loose. One of them found a shoulder and the beast changed immediately change course for Rylo. The last thing Cayden saw before losing consciousness again was the beast chasing after Rylo.  

   When Cayden he saw several fallen shrubs and branches leading away from their camp. He followed as best he could for an hour before deciding he would be of no use to Rylo with his arm crippled and some broken ribs from the feel of it.  He left the trail, head bent and heart broken, vowing to defeat this monster. He made it Iron Bay after a two week trip and headed back to his lodge Wild Woods Lodge. The Lodge fell silent when he walked in the door as the members stared in disbelief and horror at the sight of a maimed Cayden. Karn and Ike were the first two to rush over and get him to a bed. 

    After weeks of rest, and a steady diet of ale to ease the pain, he made enough of a recovery to start moving around again. Now he's putting out a bounty for any leads on the beast he thinks killed Rylo. "Information only, this kill is mine."
