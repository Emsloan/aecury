Tromokratis Aeguera is known as the Terror of the Trench, a fearsome oceanic titan creature that dwells in the deepest reaches of Aecury's seas. Possibly serving beneath the [[Heart of the Deep]] in the divine hierarchy, this creature represents the primal terror of the abyssal depths.

## Nature

Tromokratis Aeguera embodies the destructive and terrifying aspects of the deep ocean. This creature dwells where sunlight cannot reach and where the pressure would crush any normal being.

## Domains

- Abyssal depths
- Oceanic trenches
- Deep-sea mysteries
- Aquatic terror

## Legend

Tales speak of the Terror of the Trench as an ancient creature of devastating power, commanding the very waters around it and striking terror into the hearts of sailors and sea-dwellers alike.
