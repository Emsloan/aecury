Horus-re champions Shudraka, commanding him to rise up and unite the scattered beast tribes. Shudraka begins a long campaign, one by one dominating the tribes in battle. However, instead of killing each tribal leader he offers them to join him in a new and greater unified civilization. All accept, except for the race created by [[Osiris]]. Osiris is enraged by Horus-re's attempts to subjugate the other gods. In an act of defiance he leaves the pantheon and abandons his race. Horus-re is enrage by Osiris' choice and in a fit of rage commands Shudraka to exterminate the race of Osiris. Shudraka regretfully does but forever resents Horus-re's decision. Late into his life he questions the wisdom of the gods and feels deep guilt for the blood on his hands.
