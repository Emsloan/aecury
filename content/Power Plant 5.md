A long abandoned experimental power plant. It tested the feasibility of utilizing a trapped fire elemental (salamander) to generate power, storing said power in diamonds, and utilizing them as batteries.

The salamander exists in a brass chamber with a reinforced glass viewing window. The elementals are hatched from eggs placed in the chamber, emerging first as fire snakes and eventually growing into salamanders.

The chamber has two connected pipes, one on each side. The left pipe connects to a tank on a lower level filled with a pressurized gas mixture, kept in a liquid state with by ice evocation runes. A lever on the pipe can be thrown, opening the pipe. This fills the salamander's chamber with the supercooled gas, killing the salamander instantly, as a safety precaution if the salamander is ever at risk of escaping.

The right pipe leads to the room a level above where the diamonds are enchanted.

The plant was eventually overrun by kruthiks and abandoned.
