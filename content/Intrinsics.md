The Intrinsics are ageless philosophical forces that first appeared shortly after the rise of the [[Primordials]]. While the Primordials shaped Aecury's physical form, the Intrinsics manifest and shape the emotional, behavioral, and conceptual forces that govern existence.

## The Seven Intrinsics

### Maliga the Spiteful
Embodying spite and malice, Maliga created all manner of plants—grass, trees, kelp, and coral. Despite being born from spite, these creations have become essential to life on Aecury.

### Tristis the Despairing
The embodiment of despair, sorrow, and loss, Tristis governs death and endings. This Intrinsic provides the necessary counterbalance to creation and vitality.

### Phobos the Fearful
Born from fear, Phobos created night, shade, and shadow in response to Inferniax's obsession with light and revelation. These dark places provide refuge and mystery essential to existence.

### Volentia the Peaceful
Embodying love, affection, and peace, Volentia represents the softer emotions and connections that bind creatures together. Possibly responsible for the creation of animals.

### Vultus the Inquisitive
Known as a Chronopath, Vultus embodies curiosity and divination. This Intrinsic possesses the power to bend the universe to answer questions, though the First Race twisted this being in the [[Black Cradle of Vultus]].

### Mimid the Willful
The youngest Intrinsic, Mimid embodied willpower and choice. Most significantly, Mimid sacrificed themselves to grant all creatures free will by opening their wrists and bleeding their divine essence onto mortal creatures.

### Inanis the Hungry
Representing hunger and desire, Inanis remains largely mysterious, with its exact nature and creations still unknown or incomplete in the historical record.

## Characteristics

All Intrinsics are:
- Ageless and eternal
- Embodiments of emotional or behavioral forces
- Powerful enough to shape reality according to their nature
- Distinct from the physical [[Primordials]]
- Often at odds with the natural order

## Role in Creation

The Intrinsics work in concert with the [[Primordials]] to shape existence. Where Primordials created physical forms, Intrinsics infused those forms with meaning, emotion, and consciousness.

## Historical Note

According to ancient texts, the First Race sought dominion over the Intrinsics, imprisoning and warping them for their own purposes. The relationship between mortalkind and the Intrinsics remains complex and sometimes antagonistic.
