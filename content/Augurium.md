As one enters Augurium, a cold wave of air envelopes them, sending shivers down their spine. The room is dimly lit with a few flickering candles illuminating the dark corners of the chamber, and the atmosphere is heavy, suffused with an aura of mystique.

The walls of the chamber are made of rough-hewn stone, covered with lush weighty tapestries, while the floor is polished to a smooth finish, giving it a reflective quality like the surface of a still clear lake. A small pool of water encircles the platform, nearly indistinguishable from the polished stone save for the reflection of an unfamiliar star-speckled sky on it's glassy surface. The ceiling holds no such expanse.

In the center of the chamber, there is a raised platform or dais, made of black marble, and atop it sits a pedestal. The pedestal holds the ancient skull of a mind flayer. Winding runes have been etched onto every inch of its surface. When used the runes glow with green light.

The room is filled with the scent of burning incense, its smoky tendrils rising from a censer hanging from the ceiling. The aroma is a heady mix of musk and sandalwood, with a hint of something else that eludes recognition.

The chamber is quiet, all sounds muffled by the tapestries. The feeling of being watched is pervasive, and the sense of otherworldly beings present, observing from the shadows, is palpable.
