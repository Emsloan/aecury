From Orishahn's Guide:

> "The Primordials of Aecury were the unwavering building blocks of the planet, the world as it was."

> "When the world of Aecury was young in the cosmos great forces rose from its core so that life and meaning could be brought to its surface."

## The Four Primordials

- [[Inferniax the Lord of Flame|Inferniax the Violet]] - Lord of Flame (eldest and most powerful)
- [[Tannatar]] - Tannatar the Grey, Groundbreaker
- [[Cyran]] - Cyran the Teal, Master of Depths
- [[Waernah]] - Waernah the Pale, Skyborne (youngest and most frantic)

> "When the Primordials had shaped the world as they saw fit, the Intrinsics rose to look upon their creations."
