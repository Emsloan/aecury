The Primordials are the eldest and most powerful divine beings in Aecury's creation mythology. They are the physical building blocks from which the world itself was formed, rising from Aecury's core to shape the fundamental elements and structures of existence.

## The Four Primordials

### [[Inferniax]] the Lord of Flame
The eldest Primordial, Inferniax rose from Aecury's core to fill the sky with light and flame. As lord of fire and illumination, Inferniax sought to reveal all things and end all darkness. It was Inferniax who first attempted to create sentient life. Inferniax is currently imprisoned within the moon [[Iadath]].

### Tannatar the Grey
The Groundbreaker, Tannatar created the mountains, canyons, and all the great geological formations that give Aecury its physical structure and shape.

### Cyran the Teal
The Master of Depths, Cyran brought forth the oceans and filled Aecury's basins with water, creating the vast aquatic realms that cover much of the world.

### Waernah the Pale
The youngest Primordial and Skyborne, Waernah created the winds and atmosphere, providing the air that all creatures breathe and the weather systems that sustain life.

## Role in Creation

Where the [[Primordials]] shaped the physical world, the [[Intrinsics]] emerged as philosophical forces. Together, these divine beings created Aecury and continue to influence its development.

## Relationship with Other Divine Beings

The Primordials exist at a level of cosmic importance beneath [[Ao]] but above the [[Intrinsics]] and [[Elemental Titans]]. Some scholars debate whether the Primordials were themselves created by Ao or if they exist in a primordial state outside the normal divine hierarchy.
