The most widely accepted religion that is practiced within [[Historia]].
Amaunator, also known as the Golden Sun, is the primary deity for most of the peoples of Historia, with many chapels and pulpits operating in his name. Though the ruler of Historia is known as a true follower and example of [[Helm]] it is understood that every knight has a creed that they follow, and Historia's creed has been provided by Amaunator. Pontiff Ascendi is the leader of the Church of the Golden Sun, and is the primary advisor to the Rex Probus.

The Church of the Golden Sun and the [[Church of the Platinum Lord]] were the result of a schism in the Church of Wind and Sun sometime in the 23rd century.
