The leader of the [[Sandswimmers]] circa 3000.

As [[Haruspex]] of the Sandswimmers, it is Turok's duty to use divination in service of his people. Turok sacrifices creatures and burns them, inhaling the fumes to enter a trance. While entranced he can commune with the elder spirits.
