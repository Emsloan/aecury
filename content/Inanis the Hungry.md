Inanis the Hungry is one of the Intrinsics, though this philosophical force remains largely unknown or incomplete in the records of Aecury. Like the other Intrinsics, Inanis represents a particular emotional or behavioral force, but the specifics of this being's nature and influence remain shrouded in mystery.

## Nature

Hunger—whether for knowledge, sustenance, power, or something else entirely—appears to be the domain of this Intrinsic. The exact manifestations and creations of Inanis remain unclear.

## Mysteries

Much about Inanis the Hungry remains unknown. Further research into the nature of this Intrinsic and its role in the world is needed.
