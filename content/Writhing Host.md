Whaler's Cove has influx of meat. Strange group of hunters periodically come to town to sell it for dirt cheap to a local butcher. Secretly putting eggs inside, so that centipedes grow within. The people are then overridden by hunger. They come back to get more meat. Their hunger can't be satiated. Centipedes grow within their body, eating them from the inside. Eventually they feel drawn to go to the jungle in search of the freshest meats in to the cults waiting arms.

The weak are consumed, with centipedes living in their bodies making them living hives. The stronger have centipedes added to their body. Pincers, legs, 
[quote]
I feel them writhing within me. It is ecstasy.[/quote]
Original defector, rider of centipede. Forced to let his beloved mount eat of him, and he eat of them. A man swallowing a large centipede, consuming it, but the centipede bores out of his forehead. A perverse Oroboros. Cycle of consumption. Centipedes eat you, you eat centipede.

[quote]
One becomes a thousand. A thousand becomes one.[/quote]
