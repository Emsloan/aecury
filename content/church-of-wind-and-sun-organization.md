## Mythos

In the time of the Shadow Days, when darkness and malevolence held unyielding dominion, two brothers were brought into existence. The first, radiant as the dawn, bore the title of the Golden Sun. The second, gleaming with the brilliance of untarnished platinum, was the Platinum Lord.They were gods incarnate, foreordained to dispel the shadows and bestow light upon the world.
The Golden Sun grasped a scepter of purest gold, a relic that could vanquish even the deepest of shadows. The Platinum Lord wielded a blade of gleaming platinum, an instrument of justice and purity capable of cleaving through the darkest malevolence.

With resolute unity, the brothers embarked on a sacred crusade to confront the heart of darkness that had ensnared the world in the Shadow Days. Wherever they went they radiated a luminous beacon, dispersing malevolent forces that dared cross their path. Through the combined strength of their divine essences, they defeated the malevolence and cast it into the abyss from whence it had arisen.

## Origins

Following their triumph over the shadows, the brothers ascended to seats of divine power, taking their rightful places in the heavens above. Their divine artifacts were granted to their most loyal followers, and thus the first Pontiff Ascendi and Vox Platini Dei were forged. To this day the Golden Scepter and the Platinum Blade serve as symbols of hope, power, and the eternal supremacy of light over darkness.
