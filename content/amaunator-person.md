A deity worshipped in [[Historia]] within the Church of the Golden Sun .
Amaunator is the primary deity for most of the peoples of [[Historia]] , with many chapels and pulpits operating in his name. Though the ruler of [[Historia]]  is known as a true follower and example of Helm , it is understood that every knight has a creed that they follow, and [[Historia's]]  creed has been provided by Amaunator.Pontiff Ascendi  is the leader of the Church of the Golden Sun , and is the primary advisor to the Rex Probus . Many of the followers of the Golden Sun  are vocal about their faith and see themselves as the first line of defense from chaos and disorder.
