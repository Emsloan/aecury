Dormant State (Dexterity)
[ul]
[li]The wearer gains a +1 to their AC.[/li]
[li]The wearer gains a +10 to movement speed. [/li]
[li]The mantle has 1d3 charges. Whenever you fail a Dexterity save you may expend a charge and instead choose to succeed. You regain 1d3 charges at dawn.[/li]
[/ul]
Awakened State (Cunning/Hunting)
[ul]
[li]The wearer gains a +2 to their AC.[/li]
[li]Hunters Intuition: Once per day after hitting a creature with an attack roll, you can use your reaction to learn the creature's Damage Vulnerabilities, Damage Resistances, Damage Immunities, and Condition Immunities.[/li]
[li]You gain advantage on Survival checks.[/li]
[/ul]
Exalted State (Love/Healing)
[ul]
[li]The wearer gains a +3 to their AC.[/li]
[li]Sharess' Embrace: Once per day choose a creature you can see who suffers a hit. You may choose to instead receive that damage. The original target heals for half the that damage. Until your next turn allies within 30 feet of you gain advantage on Dexterity saving throws and a +10 to movement speed.[/li]
[/ul]
