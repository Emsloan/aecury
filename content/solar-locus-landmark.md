## Purpose

The Solar Locus is a forge, foundry, and smithy. Using a series of magically imbued lenses it focuses sunlight into a beam of light capable of melting and heating nearly any material. Through a complex system of levers and pullies, the lenses can be configured to provide perfect heating for any type of metalworking.
