Lolth, disguised as Osiris, revealed herself to Queen Alia Shevade the Unbroken. Offering an end to the War of Champions , she only asked that her children the Tiefling be allowed a place among the Wahashan's and that Queen Alia make some small changes to the worship rituals performed by her people.
