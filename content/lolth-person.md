The Spire is weakened, allowing Lolth to invade the material plane. Unable to withstand the Spire's influence she is expelled and takes refuge at the New World. Once there she manipulates the Yuan-ti and the Lizardfolk into allying and going to war with Wahasha. Wahasha and its gods retaliate. After a long brutal war the gods grow desperate and decide to create 5 champions for each race, instead of the usual 1. This greatly weakens them and eventually proves insufficient. In a last ditch effort they call out to their lost companion god, Osiris. Lolth, masquerading as Osiris, responds. She claims that she can end the conflict if the gods give her the power of their champions. They agree and she creates the tieflings who ride into battle and destroy the Yuan-ti and the Lizardfolk, cementing their place in the Wahashan society.

She infiltrates Wahashan society, posing as Osiris, and begins to drive the beast races apart. She uses Simic Hybrids to approach Queen Alia Tevade at the end of the War of the Champions. They claim they are avatars of Osiris and that they wish to guide the Wahashans on the path to evolution: reclaiming their original unsplit form. They also claim that the Loxodon are the closest of all the races to the true form. They convince Alia to set in motion a corruption of all the Sowing rituals, diverting power to Osiris so that she has enough power to transform the Loxodon. As a demonstration of their power they sprout wings before Alia, a certain proof of divine power.
