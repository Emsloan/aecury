Two methods of approaching the Cornerbrook, through the ocean or by land.



[h3] Land[/h3]
The party works their way through the forest to the Cornerbrook, when suddenly the player with the highest passive perception notices a deadly creature ahead of them on the path, ready to charge. Then, the person with the next highest perception hears a noise behind them: another creature! This is seemingly an unwinnable battle... RUN!

At some point during the chase one player sees a bluish blur. Glancing toward it reveals a ghost, holding a rose. The rose 

[h2]Sea[/h2]
The party decides to avoid the dangerous forest and use a boat to make their way to the beached Cornerbrook. All goes well, until they see something floating in the ocean. Make a perception check. The DC dictates how far they are when they can tell it's a body clinging to a piece of wood. When they get within 30 feet of the wood, they can tell the body is petrified. Just then, behind the body and beneath the surface they see a massive yellow shape move beneath the surface. The Swavain Basilisk attacks! 

Every time a character makes an attack roll or targets the basilisk with a spell, have them make a DC 11 perception check. On a success, they notice a paralyzed humanoid missing their legs clinging to basilisk, held up by a sword driven into its side. The sword has a cool description.
