Zemikur is one of the four duchies of [[Zemíkozešin]], the northern kingdoms. Its heraldic symbol is the Amber Wolf.

## Governance

Zemikur is ruled by [[House Tesák]], with Lord Mikolav Utíkal and Lady Justyna Utíkal serving as the current Stewards of Zemíkozešin and Champions of the Undyed Reaches. Their seat of power is Castle Utíkal.

The current scions of House Tesák are the twins Priscilla and Patrova.

## Relations with Historia

After over two years of silence between the northern kingdoms and [[Kingdom of Historia|Historia]], House Tesák extended a formal invitation to the [[Historian Syncretate|Pillars of Historia]] to attend the Feast of Jade, a celebration marking the end of summer and the coming harvests.
