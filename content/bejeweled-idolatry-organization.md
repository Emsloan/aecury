The Four Holy Seasons (Lapis, Jade, Amber, and Alabaster) is the manner in which one year is delineated by those in the Northern reaches of Akran. Each season is named after a corresponding deity whom are believed by their followers to be the founders of their homelands. The first season, Lapis, is dedicated to the “awakening” of the Lapis Bear after what are undoubtedly harsh winters. It is believed with its awakening it will bring life back to the lands of the North. The second season, Jade, is dedicated to the Jade Stag and is a time of merriment and joy. It is believed that the Jade Stag strengthens the roots of the crops planted during the season of Lapis, and grows fat and content before the “awakening” of the Amber Wolf. The third season, Amber, is dedicated to the Amber Wolf and the beginning of its hunt. It is believed that the Amber Wolf guides the farmers and hunters to healthy crops and game before the coming snows. The fourth season, Alabaster, is dedicated to the Alabaster Owl and is a time of reflection and moderation. It is believed that the Alabaster Owl cleanses the earth, “killing” her old and weak siblings and preparing them for a rebirth at the beginning of the next year.

Different regions and peoples may worship one idol above others, though each idol is recognized by most as a natural divine power. One can consider each form of worship as a separate sect, as detailed below alongside the main worshipping group:

## Tenets

Lapis Bear (Medvédal)
Strength, power, rejuvenation, spring, beginnings, fertility. Big family sizes, matriarchal. Very collectivist. Display strength and power. Proud, gregarious, loud. Survival of the fittest, the fine line between support and do it yourself. If you were on deaths door they might let you die, but if you pulled through they might aid. Spartanesque. 
Female soldiers.

Jade Stag (Alceshad)
Opulence, wealth, grandeur. Incredibly individualistic. Alchemy and artificery. Clockwork creations, steampunk without the industry, quality of life creations. Snooty. Mercantile. Whalers, ivory, ambergris. Grander the creation, the more god likes it. If you have such excess you can sacrifice it and that's really dank.

Amber Wolf (Zemikur)
Cunning, humility, comfortable but not excessive lifestyle. Doomsday preppers without the nihilism. Furries but just for dire wolves. So many fucking wolves. Hunters. Archers are held in incredibly high regard. Hunters make their own bows. Distinct bow types and styles based on families. Use the whole buffalo. Out in the open about saying don't fuck with us. 

Alabaster Owl (Desetmést) 
Welcoming, pretty cool guys, boring run of the mill basic motherfuckers. Just getting by. Cunning, humble for the purpose of survival. Backstabbing. Cunning and deceptive.
