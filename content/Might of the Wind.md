The Might of the Wind is an aerial elemental titan, representing the essence of air, movement, and the power of storms. As one of the four Elemental Titans beneath the [[Primordials]], the Might of the Wind embodies the strength and agency of Aecury's winds and atmosphere.

## Nature

This elemental titan is composed of pure wind essence and embodies the might—the raw power—of tempests, gales, and all aerial forces. It sweeps across the skies and commands the weather.

## Position in Hierarchy

The Might of the Wind serves beneath [[Waernah the Pale]], the Skyborne, while possessing its own will and purpose within the divine structure.
