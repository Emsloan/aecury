Zyz, Scourge of the Skies is a mighty aerial titan creature, possibly serving beneath the [[Might of the Wind]] in the divine hierarchy. This fearsome being terrorizes the skies above Aecury and represents a force of aerial destruction and chaos.

## Nature

Zyz embodies the destructive potential of wind and storm, standing as a testament to the raw power of the aerial realms. Few mortals have encountered Zyz and lived to tell the tale.

## Legend

As a scourge, Zyz brings calamity and terror wherever it flies. The creature's very name strikes fear into those who traverse the open skies.
