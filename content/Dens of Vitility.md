The dens of Vitility (Vih-till-eh-tee) are an enclosed bath house that have basins of steaming water strewn across the span of the structure. Exotic motifs depict acts of passion and of triumph. Of beauty and of delicacy. tldr fancy cool/ provocative art. the room is dark and steam makes it very difficult to see. There are no windows however shades in the ceiling can be opened slightly to let in natural light.
