Ossil is an artificer. 3/4 of his limbs are prosthetic. He lost them to a plague that swept his city when he was a teenager. He used to work as an archeologist, plumbing the ancient cities of Kravral. He has incorporated some of their advanced technology into his designs. He wants to support a lord with his artifice, so that they may one day use their forces to help him exact vengeance on those who had wronged him.

Ossil smokes self rolled cigarretes. He has a fire starter, which is like a zippo, which he regularly fiddles with.
