Zemíkozešin is a region north of [[Kingdom of Historia|Historia]] comprising four duchies, each represented by a heraldic animal:

| Duchy | Heraldry |
|-------|----------|
| [[Medvédal]] | The Lapis Bear |
| [[Jelenda]] | The Jade Stag |
| [[Zemikur]] | The Amber Wolf |
| [[Desetmést]] | The Alabaster Owl |

## Governance

The duchies are ruled by their respective noble houses. [[House Tesák]], rulers of [[Zemikur]], hold the title of Stewards of Zemíkozešin and Champions of the Undyed Reaches.

## Relations with Historia

Contact between Zemíkozešin and [[Kingdom of Historia|Historia]] was severed for over two years before [[House Tesák]] sent formal correspondence inviting the [[Historian Syncretate|Pillars of Historia]] to the Feast of Jade.
