**Doriak Lightbrand** - God-Emperor of the Dwarves of [[Akran]].

From Microscope session "Rise and Fall of the Dwarves of Akran":

> "Emperor Doriak Lightbrand, conquers the gnomes and unifies the dwarven kingdoms, leading to a golden age."

> "God-Emperor Doriak Lightbrand, empowered by the boon of Ferrothax, seeks other dragons and other boons to claim."

## The Codex Mutabilis

> "Doriak, nude, is in his chambers alone pouring over the spoils from their most recent raid. The words on the first page shift before his eyes, showing him the secrets of Transmutation magic."

> "Doriak's first experiment using the Codex fails, attempted to empower his kingsguard, creates crystal-nuclear fallout spreading knights"

## The Bottomless Hoard

> "Doriak creates the Bottomless Hoard as an offering for Ferrothax the Bloodscourge, cementing their bond as rider and mount."

## Betrayal of the Kings

> "God-Emperor Lightbrand holds a high council with the holdout dwarven kings. In a swift betrayal, he transmutes each into an immortal, living, metal statue. They rest in the throne room til the end of days."

## Death

> "God-Emperor Doriak Lightbrand, The Gilded, bleeds for the first time since his ascension."

> "A gnome forgotten to history, used a dagger stolen from a northern human kingdom to pierce Doriak's heart. The blade became known as the Cor Dacian."
