Jelenda is one of the four duchies of [[Zemíkozešin]], the northern kingdoms. Its heraldic symbol is the Jade Stag.

## The Feast of Jade

The Feast of Jade is a celebration held to mark the closing of summer and the coming harvests. The Dukes and Duchesses of Zemíkozešin gather for this occasion.
