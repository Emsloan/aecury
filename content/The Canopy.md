A triangular mass of long tendrilled structures that have seemingly random paths scattering in all directions. Lead to small hovels/ rooms that the denizens of this place would live. Stone mixed with fine inlay of brass and copper mix and mingle seamlessly with the entangles branches of the almost willow like trees. Upon the base of these trees there are holes burrowed seemingly random among the root systems that appear to be intentional. And between the trunks there too lingers structures of woven Sandstone and tree limbs bent in specific places.
