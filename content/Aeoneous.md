**Aeoneous, the Spiraling King** - Entity contacted in the depths by [[Maeriak Shawcloak]].

From Microscope session "Rise and Fall of the Dwarves of Akran":

> "Maeriak Shawcloak sets the record for deepest expedition, makes contact with Aeoneous, the Spiraling King."

## The Bargain

> "The Umbilim Arcanum brings Merithel's reliquary before Aeoneous, the Spiraling King. There Maeriak removes the stillborn child from his sister's corpse and offers it to Aeoneous in tribute."

> "Maeriak enters the aspect with Avraelia, and morra kills herself. A bargain is made, and Maeriak leaves the Aspect, changed, becoming Chosen of the Spiral and first of the Duergar. He pulls the Spear of Aeoneous."

## Spear of Aeoneous

> "The Chosen of the Spiral slays the false Coiled god with the Spear of Aeoneous."

> "Within the Spear lives morra Shaleplate."
