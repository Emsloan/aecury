A uniquely vast demiplane created in the long forgotten past by the original inhabitants of Aecury, containing uncountable tomes filled with lost magical secrets.

Maintained by That Which Regards, the Library eternally lies in wait until the day its treasures are reclaimed by worthy seekers of knowledge.
