The site of the fabled Gjallarmoot. The region is still host to furious elemental energies. Firestorms, choking mists, pummeling hail, and electrified earth are a few of the hazards found within. 

It seems the Jotun's wills persist, even to this day. What rage they must have felt when Geirröd refused to fall.
