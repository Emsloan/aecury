## History

The Pack has existed longer than the oldest members can recall. Its size has changed over the years but it has always existed in some form as a refuge for the unwanted.

## Culture

The Pack supports and protects its members. Many members are strong and able to fend for themselves but a fair number are not so lucky. Those unable to hunt or defend assist however they can. The Pack is a place for those who for one reason or another are not accepted in their own homes and who do not choose the life of a hermit.
