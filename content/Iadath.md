The smaller moon of planet [[Aecury]]. This lunar body has a 29 day cycle and changes inclination every year. Every 13 years it revolves in the same orbit as [[Iseolus]]. Azure in color gives the moonlight of this body a calm blue hue.

## The Prison of Inferniax

Iadath serves as the prison of [[Inferniax]], the Lord of Flame and eldest of the [[Primordials]].
