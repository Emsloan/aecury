One of three planets within its solar system, this planet is bustling with life of all types. The five continents of Zumarud , Akran , Kravral , Zavruthar , and Malna crown the oceans of this small celestial body. Two moons known as Iseolus and Iadath frequent the night skies. The oceans of Aecury are divided in to four distinct bodies of water; Thetrath Ocean , Antina Ocean ,Calcier Ocean , and Ogenora Ocean .
