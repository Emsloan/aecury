Elemental Titans are powerful divine creatures that embody the fundamental forces of Aecury's natural world. They exist in a tier of the divine hierarchy below the [[Primordials]] but are vastly more powerful than mortal beings.

## The Four Elemental Titans

Each of the four Elemental Titans corresponds to one of the primary Primordials and embodies the elemental force they represent:

### Fey/Magic Elemental
- **[[Breath of the Grove]]** - Fey magic elemental representing growth, nature, and magical vitality
  - Titan Creature: [[Hal-Yun Eiyzynh]] - The Weave Prowler

### Earth Elemental
- **[[Soul of Stone]]** - Earth elemental representing stone, mountains, and solid foundations
  - Titan Creature: [[Akupara Avolan]] - The Mountain Crusher
  - Associated Primordial: [[Tannatar the Grey]]

### Ocean Elemental
- **[[Heart of the Deep]]** - Water elemental representing oceans, depths, and aquatic power
  - Titan Creature: [[Tromokratis Aeguera]] - Terror of the Trench
  - Associated Primordial: [[Cyran the Teal]]

### Wind Elemental
- **[[Might of the Wind]]** - Air elemental representing storms, wind, and aerial power
  - Titan Creature: [[Zyz, Scourge of the Skies]]
  - Associated Primordial: [[Waernah the Pale]]

## Creature Manifestations

Each Elemental Titan has a titan creature that serves as its agent or manifestation in the world:

1. **[[Zyz, Scourge of the Skies]]** - Aerial terror under the Might of the Wind
2. **[[Tromokratis Aeguera]]** - Oceanic terror under the Heart of the Deep
3. **[[Akupara Avolan]]** - The Mountain Crusher under the Soul of Stone
4. **[[Hal-Yun Eiyzynh]]** - The Weave Prowler under the Breath of the Grove

## Role in Divine Hierarchy

The Elemental Titans serve as intermediaries between the [[Primordials]] and the rest of creation. While not as ancient or powerful as the Primordials themselves, they wield immense power and influence over their respective domains.
