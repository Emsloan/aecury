The First Pillar of [[Historia]] Chosen of [[Kelemvor]], any and all infractions of law in Historia eventually make their way up to the Iudex. Unless it has potential to be a grand/scandalous trial the Iudex Primeri has the right to assign judgment with one word: Noxium. Any criminal declared “Noxium” is immediately taken to the courtyard of Castle Historia and held on their knees at sword-point in the Cleansing Vessel until the Rex Probus can be summoned, at which point they are promptly executed and their remains are burned in the Vessel to rid Historia their evil. The Cleansing Vessel (10 ft. radius) is a massive platinum and gold bowl, only a few inches deep, at the center of the Courtyard of Castle Historia. The symbol of Amaunator is etched into the center, a golden sun with 12 points, wreathed by the two massive scaled wings of Bahamut. All around the sun are knightly figures led by Helm and skeletal figures led by Kelemvor, basking in the light of the Keeper of the Eternal Sun.
