The Heart of Bahamut's, or High Vicar's, primary duty is to legislate and uphold the teachings of [[Bahamut]] and delegates the duties of clerics within the country. They control the [[Breath of Bahamut]]. Works closely with the [[Head of Bahamut]] in times of war to preserve the fighting forces of the nation.
