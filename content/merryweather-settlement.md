The Merryweather is a massive floating city located in the Thetrath Ocean‌ between Akran‌ , Malna‌ , and Zumarud‌ . It is divided into 3 zones, and 5 layers. Each layer has two-five districts, and each zone has a different purpose in the grand machine that is The Merryweather. From full on industry to shopping and commercial, even judiciary and law enforcement, the vessel has not made it this far and expanded as much as it has without a solid system keeping the over 10,000 denizens together.
