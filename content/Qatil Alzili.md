The frontline in defending the paths first tread by the Coiled One and its purple worm kin. Identifiable by their acid-etched arms, each unique in its design. The subterranean paths, massive carved tunnels, are frequented by nomadic peoples of the [[Sandswimmers]]. While initially safe in the months following their creation, they are quickly infested by the ever persistent creatures of the Underdark. The Qatil Alzili stalk these 'halls', hunting the bizarre aberrations and harvesting their rare resources, while staking the boundaries of their people. All this, to protect [[Mawa]].
