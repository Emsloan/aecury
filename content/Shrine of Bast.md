Jist: This Pentagonal shrine has no ceiling and the walls are built of the aqueducts that feed the compound. The water cascades down the stepped interior along the vertices. Each steppe was covered in fine pillows and tapestries. In the center lay a pavilion that houses the visage of Bast wearing reveling and provocative negligee and a napping Lion lay around her heels
