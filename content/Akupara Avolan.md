Akupara Avolan is known as the Mountain Crusher, a titanic earth creature of immense strength and power. Possibly serving beneath the [[Soul of Stone]] in the divine hierarchy, this creature represents the destructive force of mountains and stone-based power.

## Nature

Akupara Avolan embodies the overwhelming might of stone and earth. This creature is so massive and powerful that entire mountains bow before its passage, and the very ground trembles at its approach.

## Domains

- Mountains
- Stone and rock
- Earth movements
- Geological transformation

## Legend

The Mountain Crusher is spoken of in awe and fear by those who dwell near mountains and high places. Some say the peaks themselves were shaped by Akupara Avolan's passage, and the creature still wanders the high places searching for worthy tests of its strength.
