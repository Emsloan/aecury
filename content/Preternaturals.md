Preternaturals are a category of beings that exist beyond the normal natural order. They occupy a position in the divine and metaphysical hierarchy, though the exact nature and extent of their powers remains largely unknown.

## Nature

Preternaturals exist beyond the scope of normal mortal comprehension and power. They may include celestials, fiends, spirits, and other entities that transcend the material plane.

## Unknown Quantity

The precise number of Preternaturals is unknown, and their individual natures and purposes remain mysterious. Some may serve the divine hierarchy under [[Ao]], while others may pursue their own agendas.

## Speculation

Whether all Preternaturals are aligned with the divine order or if some operate independently remains a matter of scholarly debate and theological uncertainty.
