A thermoluminescent violet metal, Fernaxum is commonly found within Iadathite. It is warm to the touch and grows hotter in larger quantities. Fernaxum has the peculiar quality of undergoing a powerful exothermic reaction when placed in complete darkness.

Hilts of 

Alchemists and wizards have found Fernaxum quite compatible with fire based elemental magics and thus it has seen use as a component in lanterns and more recently in armaments.
