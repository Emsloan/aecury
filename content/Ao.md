Ao is the supreme deity of Aecury, positioned at the apex of the divine hierarchy. All other divine beings—including the great [[Primordials]], the philosophical [[Intrinsics]], and the lesser gods—answer ultimately to Ao's will and dominion.

## The Supreme Authority

Ao represents the highest order and principle in existence. While the Primordials shaped the physical world and the Intrinsics embody philosophical forces, Ao encompasses all of creation and stands above all other divine entities.

## Commandments

The Commandments of Ao are written law for many of Aecury's civilizations and serve as moral and spiritual guidance across the world.

## Divine Hierarchy

Ao occupies the supreme position in the divine hierarchy, with intermediate divine realms and beings serving beneath this ultimate authority.
