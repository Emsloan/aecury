The Spire is an intermediate divine realm within Aecury's celestial hierarchy. It serves as a meeting place and realm of power for several major deities who exist below Ao in the divine order but above the mortal world.

## Inhabitants

The Spire is home to several powerful divine beings:

- [[Bahamut]] - The Platinum Lord
- [[Mystra]] - Goddess of magic and creation
- [[Asmodeus]] - Prince of Lies
- [[Mirrored One]] - An enigmatic divine entity

## Divine Function

The Spire serves as an intermediate point in the divine hierarchy, connecting Ao's supreme authority to the affairs of the mortal realm. The deities housed within The Spire have greater autonomy than lesser gods but still exist within Ao's ultimate authority.
