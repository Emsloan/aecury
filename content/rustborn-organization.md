Deserters from the [[Bellmetal Wardens]] this group of opportunistic bandits plague the burgeoning settlements of [[Historia]] and harass the myriad tribes of the Dustwalkers. They have tamed and ride Rust Monsters, using their unique ability to corrode metal to their advantage when raiding.

Their cloaks, originally pure bronze, have rusted at the bottoms over time going from crimson to orange.
