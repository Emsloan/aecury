Asmodeus, known as the Prince of Lies, is a powerful and enigmatic deity dwelling within [[The Spire]]. Despite his epithet suggesting deception and malice, Asmodeus holds a position of authority within the divine hierarchy and answers ultimately to [[Ao]].

## Nature

Asmodeus embodies cunning, deception, and manipulation. Unlike purely evil entities, Asmodeus operates within the boundaries set by Ao's authority, using lies and schemes to achieve divine purposes that may not always be apparent to mortals.

## Portfolio

- Lies and deception
- Cunning and manipulation
- Ambition
- Power through intellect

## Dwelling

Asmodeus resides in The Spire alongside other powerful deities, serving within Ao's cosmic order despite his chaotic nature.

## Paradox

The presence of the Prince of Lies within The Spire suggests that even deception and manipulation have a role to play in the cosmic order, or that Ao's authority is vast enough to encompass even this most troublesome of deities.
