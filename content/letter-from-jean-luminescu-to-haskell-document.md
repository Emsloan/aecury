Dearest Haskell,

I hope this letter finds you in warmest light.

I'm sure you have heard about my recent appointment as ambassador and my impending trip northward. From a weary Savant to a bright-eyed Aspirant, heed this sage advice: "If someone ever offers you a position in the government, run away as fast as you can." I can assure you this supposed honor has brought me nothing but headaches. If you have heard screaming in the night as of late, know that it was merely me cursing our beloved Maven Trinoa to an early grave for putting me up to this.

Putitng her damnable acts aside, Lady Zakharov and are in agreement about one thing. It is high time you begin your climb toward the rank of Postulant in earnest. Your work in translating the Kainzow tablet was nothing short of inspired, and your attempts to drown yourself in tomes about the Plane of Fire are quite impressive. An old saying of the order goes "The Postulant is forged in the verdant field". You must learn why we are known as knights, not simply dusty old bookworms. Worry not, your research will be here when you return.

To that end, I have heard of a situation in Stridobank, a town in the eastern part of the Whispering Canyons, that could use someone like you. A Duke requires aid of some sort and has put out a request for capable individuals. In my humble opinion, you should heed the call. The Whispering Canyons are home to many ancient ruins and the Dustwalkers are a varied and fascinating people. 

Go forth and seek the secrets hidden in those shadowed canyons and illuminate them for all to see.

Walk Amaunator's Light,
Jean Luminescu
Knight-Savant of the Order of the Prism
Historian Ambassador to the Undyed Reaches
