Maliga the Spiteful is one of the Intrinsics, philosophical forces that emerged after the Primordials shaped Aecury's physical form. Maliga embodies spite and created all manner of plants—grass, trees, kelp, and coral—seemingly out of pure malice and disdain. Despite the apparent cruelty of this origin, Maliga's creations have become essential to the sustenance and beauty of Aecury.

## Nature

Maliga's spite is peculiar; though driven by negative emotion, the manifestations are generative and life-giving. The plants created by this Intrinsic suggest that even from spite, creation can flow.

## Creations

- Grass
- Trees
- Kelp
- Coral
