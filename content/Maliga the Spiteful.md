**Maliga the Spiteful** - eldest and most powerful of the [[Intrinsics]].

From Orishahn's Guide:

> "The eldest and most powerful of the Intrinsics, Maliga, rose from the core of Aecury and chastised the Primordials for creating only in their image. With spite in her heart Maliga created grass and trees, as well as kelp and coral, so that the Primordials could see how they had limited themselves. With her lesson given, Maliga took the title Spiteful."
