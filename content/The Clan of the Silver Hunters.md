The Clan of the Silver Hunters:
The Tabaxi that are apart of this village all have different shades and patterns of silver fur. These Tabaxi are somewhat aware of different coats of Tabaxi but generally view there coat as a strong and powerful hunting bloodline.
Being born and raised in the Clan of the Silver Hunters means if you want to progress in title within the Clan you must complete "The Tests of Body and Soul." Generally these tests guess as follows;
1st- Tabaxi of certain bloodline at the age of 15 must test ones strength and dexterity to retrieve this blue magical flower which is used in creating a strong healing ointment. It's location is exclusivity on a peak of a mountain facing fighter's thicket on the coldest night of the year.
2nd- Those same Tabaxi that were successful in the first test now must prove there cunning prowess on the hunt and must hunt a creature that's capable of poisoning a whole village (Giant Snake near the Serpent Pillar). This act of poisoning foes to kill is a cowards way to win in this Tabaxi culture.
3rd- The Tabaxi that have completed there tests now must continue there strong bloodline to continue to grow there civilization. They must find a fitting mate and father kin. This Tabaxi must train his kittens to continue the strong bloodline and honor his father by successfully completing The Tests themselves.
Section 1 : Years as a kitten :
Sebastian growing up as an orphan in this village was tough because Sebastian must start fresh and start his own strong bloodline by completing the Tests. Sebastian found himself training to become a hunter in his clan at a young age. Sebastian trained hard and became an experienced hunter by the age of 16 after his first Test.
Section 2 : The Clans fall :
On day Sebastian came of age to go participate in the second test. When doing so he completed in killing a giant snake but his entire village was raided and skinned by an unknown evil. Feeling nothing but deviation after losing his entire way of life/family and culture had no where to go but venture and start his own village by completing the 3rd Test. This Test is normally done later in life but Sebastian must keep his culture and traditions alive. At first Sebastian felt helpless not knowing where he could even possibly meet another Tabaxi and when doing so ran into a Half-Orc Mongrel. That's where his story begins.
