The Heart of the Deep is an oceanic elemental titan, representing the essence of water, depths, and the mysteries of the sea. As one of the four Elemental Titans beneath the [[Primordials]], the Heart of the Deep embodies the power and consciousness of Aecury's oceans.

## Nature

This elemental titan is composed of pure aquatic essence and represents the heart—the vital center—of all oceanic life on Aecury. It dwells in the deepest trenches and most powerful currents.

## Position in Hierarchy

The Heart of the Deep serves beneath [[Cyran the Teal]], the Master of Depths, while maintaining its own agency within the divine structure.

## Domains

- Deep waters
