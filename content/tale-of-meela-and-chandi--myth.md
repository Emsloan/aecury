One day two brothers walked along. The younger suggested they rest and the elder agreed, leading them to a high hill. As they rested, the elder brother stared up at the sweeping blue expanse of the sky. The elder brother was the greedy sort and declared he wanted to walk amongst the clouds. The younger brother, slovenly and timid, thought to stop his brother for his hubris but instead fell asleep on the warm grass.

After a time, the younger heard whoops and screams of excitement and joy. He opened his eyes and looked to the source of the noise. High in the sky he saw his brother flying about, huge black wings sprouted where is arms had once been. The younger brother felt fear, for he knew that the sky was not the domain of the people. He thought to tell his older brother this but feared retaliation and held his tongue. The two brothers went home, the elder making the younger promise to keep his flight a secret.

The next day the two brothers were again walking about and the younger suggested a rest. The elder pointed to the hill they had visited the day before. They rested for a time. Again, the elder brother looked to the sky and peered at the midday sun burning brightly high above him. He declared he wanted to walk upon it.
