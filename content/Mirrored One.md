The Mirrored One is an enigmatic deity dwelling within [[The Spire]], whose nature and purpose remain largely mysterious. Little is known about this being beyond its presence among the other major deities that serve [[Ao]].

## Mystery

The Mirrored One's epithet suggests reflection, duality, or perhaps the ability to show mortals their true selves. The exact domains and powers of this deity remain shrouded in mystery.

## Dwelling

The Mirrored One resides in The Spire, indicating a position of significance within the divine hierarchy, though the full extent of its authority and influence is unknown.

## Speculation

Scholars and theologians debate whether the Mirrored One represents:
- Reflection and truth
- Duality and balance
- Hidden aspects of self
- Something beyond current mortal comprehension
