House Tesák is the ruling noble house of [[Zemikur]], one of the four duchies of [[Zemíkozešin]]. The current lord and lady are Mikolav Utíkal and Justyna Utíkal, who hold the titles of Stewards of Zemíkozešin and Champions of the Undyed Reaches.

## Seat of Power

The house rules from Castle Utíkal.

## Scions

The heirs of House Tesák are the twins Priscilla and Patrova. Both are humans in their mid-30s with bright green eyes that rarely blink and bright smiles that some find off-putting. They typically wear matching dark gray-green tunics with amber colored accents. Priscilla has short, cropped hair just above the cheekbones, while Patrova has a very short, tidy buzz cut.
