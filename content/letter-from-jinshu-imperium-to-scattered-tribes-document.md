## Contents

To the esteemed leaders and people of the Northern Tabaxi Tribes of Zumarud,
It is with great pleasure that we, the Jinshu Imperium, extend our warm greetings to you. It is our fervent hope that this correspondence finds you in good health and spirits.

As a great nation with a long and storied history, we recognize the importance of building strong and mutually beneficial relationships with other nations. It is with this in mind that we seek to establish formal ties with your esteemed peoples.

In recent years, our lands have been embroiled in conflict with the neighboring Beasthold and Oroboros. The War of Champions has drawn on too long and the Jinshu people seek a swift and decisive end.

We are aware of the complicated relationship between your nation and Wahasha, and so would not be so foolish as to suggest an outright alliance. Instead, we seek simply to plant the seeds of an era of peace and prosperity, built on mutual respect and understanding between our nations. We believe that by working together, we can create a brighter future for our peoples, one that is free from these sorts of vile conflicts.

This missive is delivered by our ambassador, Wen Gu, and his scribe, Peng Jinzi. We hope that you will treat our delegation as honored guests and that you enjoy this small sample of the riches the Imperium possesses.

Sincerely,

Lixiang de Gu
Most Holy Emperor of the Jinshu Imperium
