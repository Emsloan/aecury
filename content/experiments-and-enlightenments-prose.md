## Prose

A grayish colored book with the symbol of the Onyx Gaze pressed into it. A log of Lusgidall’s experiments on humanoids and non-humanoids who showed promise for becoming the ‘next step of illithid-kin’. 
> “The monsters of these wastes are strong vessels but lack the fortitude I seek. Humanoid spirit is resilient and stubborn, but once broken the vessel is imbued with the same stubbornness. I must convince the other arcane users to aid in my experiments.”

They theorize that a younger host with a more concentrated stasis fluid to aid in the nutrients needed by the tadpole would allow for the tadpole to mature with the host and create a symbiotic relationship, granting greater powers to the subject, after a few generations the need for a tadpole may disappear. Perhaps if the tadpole retained its connection to him he could take control when needed. It shows a log of 35 total experiments, 1-30 have FAILED written over top of them in red ink, the successful experiments each have an ‘assignment’ section. #31 was sent to infiltrate ’the old world’. #32 was sent to ‘rise in the ranks of the “Court of the Mirrored One”’. #33 has a large gash next to the number, like a knife was plunged into the page, and in red ink it says ESCAPED next to it. #34 and #35 have no additional information.
