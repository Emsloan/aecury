The Unsleeping Eyes are the vigilant and dedicated martial members of the [[Church of the Iron Guard]]. As the name suggests, they are ever-watchful, ensuring the safety and security of [[Amangrad]] and its inhabitants. Their unwavering commitment to their duty is inspired by the teachings of [[Helm]], emphasizing Honor, Duty, Vigilance, and Preparedness.

Making up the city guards and the majority of the military, the Unsleeping Eyes are the first line of defense against any threats to [[Amangrad]] and the [[Historian Syncretate]]. They are not just warriors but also the moral compass of the city, ensuring that the tenets of Helm are upheld in every corner. Their presence is a constant reminder of the importance of duty and honor.

Every member of the Unsleeping Eyes undergoes rigorous training, both physical and spiritual. They are taught the history of Historia, the teachings of Helm, and the importance of their role in the city. This training ensures that they are not only formidable in combat but also steadfast in their beliefs.

The Unsleeping Eyes hold the Rex Probus in the highest regard, seeing them as the living embodiment of Helm's teachings. They believe that the Rex Probus, through their honorable actions and unwavering duty, is the closest mortal representation of Helm. This deep respect ensures that the Unsleeping Eyes are fiercely loyal to the Rex Probus, ready to lay down their lives in their service.

The presence of the Unsleeping Eyes, combined with the lawful tenets of the Golden Sun, ensures that crime is a rarity in Historia. They are not just enforcers but also educators, teaching the citizens the importance of honor and duty. Their influence is felt in every aspect of life in Historia, from the markets to the royal palace.

Any transgressions against the peace of Historia are met with swift and severe consequences. The Unsleeping Eyes, in conjunction with the laws of the Golden Sun, ensure that justice is served promptly. Their reputation for fairness, combined with their formidable presence, acts as a deterrent for any potential wrongdoers.

They have a rivalry with the [[Bellmetal Wardens]].
