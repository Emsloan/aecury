When the armies of [[Historian Syncretate]] threatened @Helancroft(settlement) many brave men sacrificed their lives to defend their home. When those same men's corpses rose again under Historia's banner, the women of Helancroft were forced to trade spindle for axe. For 20 days the villagers held out against the undead siege until only 40 women remained in the abbey. As the horde splintered the innermost doors, the setting sun bathed the survivors in a warm red glow. The doors gave way and the dead swarmed The women fought all through the night until the bodies of the dead piled high, and thus Ilmatra was born.
