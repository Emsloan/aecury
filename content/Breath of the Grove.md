The Breath of the Grove is a Fey/magic elemental titan, representing the essence of nature, growth, and magical vitality. As one of the four Elemental Titans beneath the [[Primordials]], the Breath of the Grove embodies the fey magic that permeates wild places and natural growth.

## Nature

This elemental titan is composed of pure fey magic and natural essence. It represents the breath—the life force—that animates all growing things in Aecury's forests, groves, and wild places.

## Position in Hierarchy

The Breath of the Grove serves beneath [[Maliga the Spiteful]] and possibly other Intrinsics, while remaining connected to the overall divine structure through the Primordials.

## Manifestations

The titan's presence can be felt in magical glades, enchanted forests, and places where the boundary between the mortal and fey realms grows thin.
