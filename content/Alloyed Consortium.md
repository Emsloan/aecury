The Alloyed Consortium refers to the four distinct city-states in the Frontier region of [[Kravral]]. The city-states are controlled by any number of merchant princes, who in turn each control unique industries that the other city-states rely on. The Alloyed Consortium presents a unified front to the other nations of the world, but are internally divided more than they'd like to admit. The city-states that make up the Consortium are:

[ul]
[li]Deadcity: Ruled by Lirael the Hollow, who rules in undeath and her many cloned 'daughters' who all vie for control. Everything is done by undead labor.[/li]
[li]Steamcity: A city that harnesses captured elementals and traps them to power devices. Conjuration magic is used to pull elementals. Many of the peoples are genasi from long exposure to elemental energies.[/li]
[li]Faedruidcity: A city that long ago received a transplant of a powerful tree. The people who live here are very intuned with nature and the barrier between here and the fae is very thin.[/li]
[/ul]
There lives another distinct group within this region of Kravral called the Unmoored Union. This group is made up of dwarves and giants and live as nomadic traders. They travel the dangerous wastelands between cities, trusted as a reliable third party. Not much is known about the origin of the Unmoored, but folk tales tell of a time when they were entrenched in a bloody war long ago.
