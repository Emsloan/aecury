One of the five continents of [[Aecury]] . This land is home to the nations of [[EverRealm]] , [[Historian Syncretate]] , and the [[Crimson Province]] . The northern regions of this continent contain untamed lands that are known as the [[Savage Lands]] . There is only one known village within the [[Savage Lands]]  known as [[Lunis]] .
