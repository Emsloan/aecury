After the canonization of the [[Forgewielder]] a small but quickly growing following took root in [[Historian Syncretate]]. The Fraternity considers itself as a sect of the [[Church of the Platinum Lord]]. It is led by the Platinum-touched prophet, [[Theobold the Crucible]], who claims to have known the Forgewielder in life. The sect preaches humility, good acts, faith, and acceptance.
