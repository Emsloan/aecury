Two yuan-ti malison have been attacked by 4 bandits and their leader, [[Urister Proudbrand]].

The bandits noticed the light of campfires near the abandoned outpost the previous night. Scouting, they saw the yuan-ti camping out and assumed they were mindless monsters. They planned an ambush for the next day.

The bandits come from a fort to the west.
