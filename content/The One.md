**The One** is **Alasadriel**, a god created by [[The First Race]] (the elves) through apotheosis.

A titan-like figure depicted in murals.

## From Orishahn's Guide - Mural Description

> "There is only one figure now whose features you can make out amongst a sea of smaller forms who all have their hands raised and heads bent back towards the sky. This figure is depicted being about as tall as Clavis or the rider on the wall, but when scaled to the people at their feet, they stand above the peaks and clouds."

> "It's a more feminine form though no gender is discernible, wearing a flowing robe with millions of intricacies and patterns."

> "[INVESTIGATION 15] the patterns are thousands and thousands of arcane formulas, so many that your eyes can't even pick out where one might end and another begin"

> "They have a circlet on their head with eight star-like gems embedded in it. Floating above one of their palms is a perfectly spherical object, with their reflection seen in it, above their other palm is a ring, about as tall as their torso, with even more intricate patterns on it, along the rim are dozens and dozens of circular indentations."
