In ages past, a Great Evil stole Truth from the heavens and locked it away in a vault. The Great Evil then split the vault in 3 pieces, Vault, Lock, and [[Blackvane]]. Legend states that, should the pieces be reunited, the Truth will be revealed. Legends also say that the Truth is vulnerable, and can be destroyed only when the pieces are together. Cults of evil seek the pieces to destroy the Truth in service of their evil masters.
