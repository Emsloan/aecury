The Church of Wind and Sun was an ancient religion, with some preserved records and relics dating back to the later years of the 1st millenium. Following the time of [[Great Flood]], the church experienced a schism and split into the [[Church of the Golden Sun]] and the [[Church of the Platinum Lord]].

The exact reasons for the schism are not well known as little remains from those chaotic days.
