**Codex Mutabilis** - Artifact of transmutation magic, originally gnomish.

From Microscope session "Rise and Fall of the Dwarves of Akran":

> "The dwarves steal the Codex Mutabilis from the Gnomes"

> "The words on the first page shift before his eyes, showing him the secrets of Transmutation magic."

## Treaty

> "Doriak mends relations with the gnomes, ending the age of squabbles. A contract stating that the Codex would be given to the gnomish leader upon Doriak's death."

> "Doriak recognizes the power and danger the Codex holds. He tries to ensure the power will shift when his time is done. Merithel fears this causes them harm by painting a target on them. Merithel convinces Doriak to allow her access to the Codex, with intent to master it."
