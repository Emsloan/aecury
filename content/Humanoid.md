The majority of intelligent creatures on [[Aecury]] are humanoids. Generally, they have two legs, two arms, and one head. Beyond those shared similarities, the types of forms and traits humanoids possess are incredibly varied. It is presumed that all humanoids can interbreed, though some half-breeds are more common than others.
