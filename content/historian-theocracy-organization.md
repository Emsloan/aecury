## History

Founded in 2386 by Marceli Janozyk Historia, the small kingdom quickly rose to prominence by bringing many smaller tribes into their fold. The first major victory won by the armies of Historia was in 2401 when Historia ended a 3 year war with the northern nation of Kelaemvalk. This was also when the god [[Kelemvor]]  was introduced to the populace by way of slaves taken in the conflict.

## Culture

The kingdom of Historia, located in the eastern province of Akran, is ancient and well established. One unique aspect of the throne in Historia is that it has been, since its inception, dedicated to the one who most perfectly represents the ideals of Helm. Inside the walls of the city one will find that many are devout followers of the four faiths, and crime is all but a bad dream. Historia has a long history of victorious battles with small city states that may rise up in the east, and for that they claim much of the area surrounding the high walls of the city.
