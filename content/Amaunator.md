A deity worshipped in [[Historian Syncretate]] within the [[Church of the Golden Sun]].
Amaunator is the primary deity for most of the peoples of [[Historian Syncretate]] , with many chapels and pulpits operating in his name. Though the ruler of [[Historian Syncretate]] is known as a true follower and example of [[Helm]] , it is understood that every knight has a creed that they follow, and [[Historian Syncretate]] creed has been provided by Amaunator. The [[Pontiff Ascendi]] is the leader of the [[Church of the Golden Sun]] , and is the primary advisor to the [[Rex Probus]] . Many of the followers of the [[Church of the Golden Sun]] are vocal about their faith and see themselves as the first line of defense from chaos and disorder.
