Hal-Yun Eiyzynh is known as the Weave Prowler, a mysterious and powerful creature associated with magic, mysteries, and the hidden currents of power that flow through Aecury. Possibly serving beneath the [[Breath of the Grove]] or connected to the fey realms in the divine hierarchy, this creature embodies magic and arcane mystery.

## Nature

Hal-Yun Eiyzynh represents the ephemeral and mysterious aspects of magical power. This creature prowls the boundaries between the material and magical realms, seeking to understand and manipulate the flows of arcane energy.

## Domains

- Magic and the arcane
- The weave of reality
- Fey realms and boundaries
- Mystical power

## The Weave

The creature is called the Weave Prowler because it travels along the mystical currents of magic that bind all of Aecury together. It understands these currents in ways that mortal mages can only dimly comprehend.

## Legend

Those who study the arcane speak of Hal-Yun Eiyzynh with equal parts reverence and wariness. The creature is said to reward those who respect the deeper mysteries but to punish those who abuse magical power recklessly.
