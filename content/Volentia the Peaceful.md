Volentia the Peaceful is one of the Intrinsics, a philosophical force embodying love, affection, and peace. In contrast to the harsher aspects of other Intrinsics, Volentia brings gentleness and connection to Aecury. This Intrinsic is possibly responsible for the creation of animals, infusing them with capacity for companionship and bond.

## Nature

Volentia represents the softer emotions and connections that bind creatures together. Love, affection, and the bonds between living things are expressions of Volentia's influence.

## Possible Creations

- Animals (hypothesis)
- The capacity for love and affection

## Portfolio

- Love
- Affection
- Peace
- Companionship
