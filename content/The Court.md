Remaining original elves ([[The First Race]]) who came to the [[Yellow Wastes]] after [[Ao]]'s glassing, now long warped and twisted by the nature of [[Mimid the Willful|Mimid]]/[[Mirrored One|Mirrored Ones]]/Yellow Kings/[[Yellow Wastes]].

Imprisoned in the Yellow Wastes. Characters adapted from MCDM books.

## Members

From Orishahn's Guide (spoken by an unnamed narrator):

> "I'm not sure, it may have been Uursovk, the most scholarly of the court members. They often were the one telling me stories of the old world and how they had made some discovery that would change the universe."

> "Then Maladar would step in and tell me that the past is behind us, the only thing we can do now is look for a future that we create ourselves... He wasn't always an ass."

> "Most disagreements within the court were between Maladar and Rall, or Lord Rall is what they referred to themselves as, they and Uursovk were the ones I spent my free time with."

> "Those two had helped instill in me a love for this place, told me to appreciate it as my home. Malgas was a killjoy but they too had a passion for this place and its denizens."

> "The rest however, well let's just say they were want to relive the glory days."

### The Queen

> "she isn't actually the Queen, I've just never heard her or anyone else refer to her as otherwise, so it has become her name I guess."

The Queen apparently defaced some of the murals.

## Their Imprisonment

> "If I had to count the number of times they claimed we should find some sort of hole in the defenses of those who locked them here, well I don't think I could to be frank"

> "Do you know what tentacle-faces plan is? Aside from forcing our hands to murder of course."

**"Tentacle-face"** refers to [[Luzgidall]] or another member of the [[Onyx Gaze]] - a mind flayer group that lived/ruled in the Yellow Wastes. Luzgidall (the final one) was killed by [[Ivory Masquerade]].
