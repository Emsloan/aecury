The site where a Voninjotunen, or titan, died near the end of the Esprin-Jotun War. Its massive ribcage creates a natural frame, like wooden beams in a home. This prefabricated structure was not missed by the Righteous Dregs who converted them into a camp reminiscent of a carnival tent. 

The skull has been turned into a trove and the place where the bandit lord lives. Each hand has also been turned into a smaller tend-like structure.
