Town criers proclaim "Beware! Bandits plague the Kingsroad!" Merchants and travelers have been attacked traveling to the frontier outposts. Those heading that direction are encouraged to hire more guards. The government strongly wants these bandits dealt with so more people move to the frontier. Strangely, the bodies and carts recovered still have their coin.
