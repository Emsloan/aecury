Bahamut, also known as the Platinum Lord, is a major deity dwelling within [[The Spire]]. As one of the divine beings positioned directly under [[Ao]] in the celestial hierarchy, Bahamut wields considerable power and influence over the affairs of Aecury.

## The Platinum Lord

Bahamut is associated with platinum and metallic perfection, representing order, justice, and good against the chaos and selfishness that Bahamut opposes. Many metalworkers and those devoted to righteous causes look to Bahamut for guidance.

## Dwelling

Bahamut resides in The Spire, the intermediate divine realm that hosts several of [[Ao]]'s most powerful servants.

## Aspects

- Justice
- Order
- Metallic perfection
- Divine good
