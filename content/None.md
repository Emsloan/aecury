## Prose

Jet black covered book with the symbol of the Onyx Gaze pressed into it. Written in a script that differs from the gray tome. The first page states that:
> “My onyx gaze will bring all to their knees, and all who do not bend to my will shall know its pain, decreed by Zagrudull, King of the Onyx Gaze”.

Discusses the discovery of doorways to other places on this plane that can be opened through dark arcane means that require a ‘crimson key’ which is described as the spilling of a pint of mortal blood and “painting the air with it”. Zagradull theorizes that doorways to other ‘spaces’ may need more potent keys, or keys that originate from those spaces. Finally, before getting to a log of experiments with the doorways and successful usages, they pontificate on how no other creature could have made this discovery, for they are all powerful and all knowing, and they will subjugate the world with this eldritch knowledge of the universe.
