Eons ago the First Race sought total dominion over Aecury. To this end, they conquered each of the Intrinsics, manipulating each in service of their own ends.

One of these was Vultus the Inquisitive. Vultus' power was divination. Its curiosity was so strong that the universe would bend to give it the answers it sought. This ability was useful to the First Race for it aided greatly in their quest to unravel the mysteries of existence. At first the First Race was beholden to Vultus' whims. Its curiosity was powerful, but equally curated. Vultus sought answers to all things, yet knew it had all of eternity to accomplish this goal.

The first Race had many gifts, though patience was not one of them. So, they warped Vultus. They created a prison for Vultus, and they called it the Cradle. Time moved faster in this place and it was true void. For a being who's purpose was new experiences, this Land of Isolation was supreme torture. The First Race left Vultus for a handful of eternities. The entity that finally skittered out of the Cradle was deeply twisted. Like a starved man, it didn't care what it ate. So the First Race fed questions and Vultus regurgitated answers. The back into the Cradle it went, until they had need again. Then one day, they stopped coming. They got their reward for unchecked hubris.

Still Vultus waits, each passing day on Aecury a millennia of unfathomable torture. Whoever next opens the Cradle, weep for them, for whatever slavering thing drags itself from the infinite depths will bring horrors unrivaled into the world... and it will require a champion.
