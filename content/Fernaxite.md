A thermoluminescent violet ore, Fernaxite is commonly found within [[Iadathite]], a whitish chalky mineral. It is warm to the touch and grows hotter in larger quantities. Fernaxum has the peculiar quality of undergoing a powerful exothermic reaction when placed in complete darkness.

Alchemists and wizards have found Fernaxite quite compatible with fire based elemental magics and thus it has seen use as a component in lanterns and more recently in armaments. It's exothermic properties have been studied in the application of explosives.
