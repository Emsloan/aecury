**Inferniax the Lord of Flame** is the eldest of the [[Primordials]], the divine beings who rose from [[Aecury]]'s core to shape the fundamental elements of the world. As lord of fire and illumination, Inferniax sought to reveal all things and end all darkness. It was Inferniax who first attempted to create sentient life.

## Imprisonment

Inferniax is currently imprisoned within the moon [[Iadath]].
