A dense and dangerous island has eluded colonization for many years as those who had tried were unable to withstand the brutality and unforgiveness of the Jungles beyond the shore. Lurking behind every tree and vine are all manner of beast and plant that, if given the chance, will hunt and kill. Many nations that have passed from memory, never to be recollected, have attempted and failed to survive on this primal and hungry landscape.

Some 50 years ago the nation known as @[EverRealm](location:80f575b0-7ae1-4a6a-993f-21aaa1aee153)‌ set it sites on this island and since then has tried relentlessly to harness resources from this untapped land. Many ships were sent, some just to circumvent the island, others to land in small groups and survive. For 35 years none of these ship ever made it around the island, and no colonizers made it through a year. Either through death or cowardice those tasked with surviving, failed.

That is until 15 years ago, a young and hopeful gnomish man named Samson completed the first ever full circumvention of the island and he and his crew learned something that ever since, has been kept secret. This was no island, but an entire continent whose circumvention tallied over 2400 nautical miles and took over three weeks of constant sailing to reach the point at which reached northmost. After this long voyage Samson and his crew had no choice but to land and restock.
