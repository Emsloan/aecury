# Mimid Research

## What We Know (from sources)

### Names/Titles
- "Mimid the Willful" (Orishahn's Guide)
- "Mimid the Wanderer" (Inferniax article from WA)
- "(Maverick)" - note in Orishahn's Guide

### Role as Intrinsic
From Orishahn's Guide:
> "Finally the youngest and most fleeting of the Intrinsics, Mimid, rose from the core to look upon the creations of their siblings and the Primordials."

### The Sacrifice
From Orishahn's Guide:
> "Much to the surprise of all the greater powers Mimid saw the world they had made and wept... With a heavy heart Mimid opened their wrists and began to travel the world, granting the creatures of Aecury the gift of their blood and Will"

From Inferniax article (WA export):
> "Mimid sliced their palm and let the hot blood flow freely onto the clay creations. They gorged themselves on Mimid's essence, itself an amalgam of pure desire."

### Pact with Elves
From Inferniax article:
> "he made a pact with the nascent elves. Regardless of time or circumstance, they could call Mimid's domain their own."

### The 9-Day Battle
From Inferniax article:
> "Mimid was mocking in their response, as was their way. This enraged Inferniax, causing his violet flames to sear bright. Mimid did as his nature demanded, responding in kind with golden flames of even greater brilliance."
> "The two raged in this way for 9 days"

Note: Mimid has **golden flames**.

## Answered Questions

1. **How does Mimid transition from Intrinsic to one of Ao's "big 4"?**
   → After Ao glassed Aecury, Ao brought Asmodeus, Mystra, and Bahamut from other realms and "upgraded" Mimid to be part of the Big 4. Mimid is still an Intrinsic.

2. **What are Mimid's other names/aspects?**
   → Mimid the Willful, Mimid the Wanderer, (Maverick), **Mirrored One**

4. **Does Mimid occupy a position in The Spire?**
   → Yes, as the Mirrored One (Chaos) alongside Bahamut (LG), Mystra (LN), Asmodeus (LE)

5. **What is the connection between Mimid's sacrifice and the Mirrored One?**
   → They are the same entity. Mirrored One = Mimid.
